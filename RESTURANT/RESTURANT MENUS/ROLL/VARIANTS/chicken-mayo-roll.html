Chicken Mayo Roll

The Chicken Mayo Roll is a delightful and easy-to-make snack, perfect for a quick lunch or as a light dinner option. It consists of tender chicken pieces mixed with creamy mayonnaise, wrapped in a soft roll, often accompanied by lettuce or other fresh vegetables. It's a favorite for its simplicity and delicious taste.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon mustard sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery (optional)
  • 4 soft rolls or tortillas
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken with mayonnaise and mustard sauce. Add salt and pepper to taste. Mix well until the chicken is evenly coated.
  2. If using, stir in the chopped celery for added crunch and flavor.
  3. Take each roll or tortilla and line it with lettuce leaves.
  4. Spoon the chicken mixture onto the lettuce leaves.
  5. Roll up the tortillas or close the rolls, securing them with a toothpick if necessary.
  6. Serve immediately or wrap in foil to take on the go.
