Beef Biryani

Beef Biryani is a savory and aromatic rice dish popular in South Asian cuisine. It's a rich and flavorful meal that combines marinated beef, basmati rice, and a blend of spices, making it a delightful feast for any occasion.

Ingredients:

For the Beef Marinade:

  • 500g beef, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste

For the Biryani:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2-3 green cardamoms
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the beef with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Let it rest for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.
  2. Wash and soak the basmati rice in water for 30 minutes.
  3. In a large pot, heat the oil and fry the onions until golden brown. Set aside half for garnishing.
  4. Add cumin, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, and bay leaf to the remaining onions in the pot. Fry for a minute.
  5. Add the marinated beef and cook until it's browned and tender.
  6. Add tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  7. In another pot, boil water and cook the rice until it's about 70% done. Drain the water.
  8. Layer the cooked beef and the par-cooked rice in the pot. Sprinkle the fried onions, and coriander leaves on top.
  9. Cover and simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked.
  10. Garnish with the reserved fried onions and serve hot.
