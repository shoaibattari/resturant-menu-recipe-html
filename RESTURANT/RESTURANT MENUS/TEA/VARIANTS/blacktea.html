Black Tea

Black tea is a robust and flavorful beverage enjoyed worldwide. It's made from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant.

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Black tea leaves
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Milk (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a kettle or a saucepan.
  2. Add black tea leaves according to taste preference.
  3. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes (adjust time for desired strength).
  4. Remove the tea leaves or strain the tea.
  5. Add sugar if desired.
  6. Add milk for a creamy texture (if preferred).
  7. Enjoy your freshly brewed black tea!
