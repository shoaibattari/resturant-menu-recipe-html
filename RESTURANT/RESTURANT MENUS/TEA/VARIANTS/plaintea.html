Plain Tea

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Tea leaves
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Milk (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a kettle or a saucepan.
  2. Add tea leaves according to taste preference.
  3. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes on low heat.
  4. Add sugar if desired.
  5. Add milk for a creamy texture (if preferred).
  6. Strain into cups and serve hot.
