Chicken Qorma

Chicken Qorma is a rich and creamy dish that is a staple in South Asian cuisine. Known for its aromatic blend of spices, yoghurt, and tender chicken pieces, this dish offers a delightful and indulgent experience, perfect for special occasions or a comforting meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups yogurt, whisked
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 4-5 green cardamoms
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil or ghee and fry the onions until golden brown. Remove the onions and grind them into a paste.
  2. In the same pot, add the ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and fry until they turn white.
  4. Add coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add the onion paste back into the pot along with the yogurt. Stir well.
  6. Add cardamoms, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Cover and cook on low heat until the chicken is tender and the oil separates.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala powder and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
