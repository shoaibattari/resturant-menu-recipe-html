Beef Qorma

Beef Qorma is a luxurious and aromatic dish deeply rooted in South Asian culinary traditions. This rich stew combines tender beef with a mix of fragrant spices and creamy yogurt, creating a hearty and flavorful experience ideal for festive occasions or indulgent meals.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef, cut into cubes
  • 2 cups yogurt, whisked
  • 2 large onions, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 4-5 green cardamoms
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil or ghee in a large pot and fry the onions until golden brown. Remove the onions and grind them into a paste.
  2. In the same pot, add the ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add the beef cubes and fry until they change color.
  4. Mix in coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add the onion paste back to the pot along with the yogurt. Stir well.
  6. Include cardamoms, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Cover and cook on low heat until the beef is tender and the oil starts to separate from the gravy.
  7. Sprinkle with garam masala powder and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
