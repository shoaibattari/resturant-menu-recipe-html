Green Tea

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Green tea leaves
  • Honey or sugar (optional)
  • Lemon (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a kettle or saucepan.
  2. Once boiled, let the water cool for a minute or two (to around 80°C/175°F).
  3. Add green tea leaves to a teapot or mug (1 teaspoon per cup).
  4. Pour the hot water over the tea leaves.
  5. Let it steep for 2-3 minutes for a mild flavor or up to 5 minutes for a stronger taste.
  6. Strain the tea to remove leaves.
  7. Add honey or sugar for sweetness (if desired) and a squeeze of lemon for a citrusy twist.
  8. Enjoy your refreshing green tea!
