Chicken Burger

The Chicken Burger is a delightful twist on the classic burger, featuring a juicy and flavorful chicken patty, fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and a creamy sauce, all sandwiched between soft burger buns. It's a perfect meal for those who love a lighter yet satisfying burger experience.

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 4 burger buns
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 4 cheese slices (optional)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the ground chicken, onion, garlic, paprika, oregano, salt, pepper, egg, and breadcrumbs until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into 4 burger patties.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and cook the patties for about 5 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and golden brown.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly on a pan.
  5. To assemble the burgers, spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun. Place a lettuce leaf, a slice of tomato, the chicken patty, and optionally a slice of cheese.
  6. Cover with the top half of the bun and serve immediately.
