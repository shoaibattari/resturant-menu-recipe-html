Zinger Burger

The Zinger Burger is a popular fast-food item known for its crispy, spicy chicken fillet, served in a soft burger bun. This burger combines the crunchiness of the chicken coating with the tenderness of the meat, alongside fresh lettuce and a creamy mayonnaise-based sauce, creating a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts, pounded to even thickness
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Oil for frying
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Mayonnaise
  • Slices of cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour with paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs and buttermilk.
  3. Dip each chicken breast first in the egg mixture, then in the flour mixture, ensuring it's well-coated. Repeat for a double coating.
  4. Heat the oil in a deep fryer or a large pan to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry the chicken breasts until golden brown and cooked through, usually about 5-7 minutes per side.
  6. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  7. To assemble the burger, spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun. Place a lettuce leaf, followed by the fried chicken, and if desired, a slice of cheese.
  8. Cover with the top half of the bun and serve hot.
