Beef Burger

The Beef Burger is a classic favorite, featuring a juicy and flavorful beef patty, topped with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and a savory sauce, all nestled within a soft, toasted bun. It's a timeless dish that's loved for its rich taste and satisfying texture, perfect for grilling season or a hearty meal any time of the year.

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef (80% lean meat, 20% fat)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 burger buns, split and toasted
  • 4 cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, or burger sauce
  • Oil for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  2. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, and form into 4 evenly sized patties.
  3. Grill the patties for about 4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or longer for desired doneness.
  4. If using cheese, add a slice to each patty during the last minute of grilling and cover the grill to let the cheese melt.
  5. Assemble the burgers by placing a beef patty on each bun bottom. Add lettuce, tomato slices, and onion as desired.
  6. Add ketchup, mustard, or your preferred burger sauce.
  7. Cover with the bun tops and serve immediately.
