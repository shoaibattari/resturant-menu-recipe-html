Chicken Crispy Roll

Chicken Crispy Roll is a delicious snack or appetizer featuring a crispy outer layer wrapped around a flavorful chicken filling. This dish combines the crunchiness of the roll's coating with the tenderness of the seasoned chicken, making it a delightful treat for gatherings or as a quick snack option.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat some oil and saut√© the onions until translucent. Add the minced garlic and ginger paste, and cook for another minute.
  2. Add the finely chopped chicken and cook until it's no longer pink.
  3. Season the chicken with red chili powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook until the chicken is fully cooked and the flavors are infused.
  4. Take a spring roll wrapper and place a spoonful of the chicken mixture at one end of the wrapper.
  5. Fold the sides over the filling and roll it up tightly, sealing the edges with a little water.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the rolls until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove from the oil and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
