Chicken Chatni Roll

The Chicken Chatni Roll is a popular and flavorful street food item, combining the spiciness of marinated chicken with the tangy taste of chatni, all wrapped in a soft flatbread. It's an ideal snack for those who crave a quick yet mouth-watering meal, combining the rich flavors of Indian cuisine in an easy-to-eat format.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 4 paratha or tortillas
  • 1 cup green chutney (mint and coriander)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat oil and add the shredded chicken. Sprinkle red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Saut√© until the chicken is well coated and heated through.
  2. Warm the parathas or tortillas in a separate pan.
  3. Spread a generous amount of green chutney over each paratha or tortilla.
  4. Add a portion of the spiced chicken, onion slices, and tomato slices on top of the chutney.
  5. If desired, sprinkle some grated cheese over the filling.
  6. Roll the parathas or tortillas tightly to enclose the filling. If needed, secure with a toothpick.
  7. Serve the Chicken Chatni Rolls hot, accompanied by extra green chutney for dipping.
